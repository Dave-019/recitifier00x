My First Blog: Hidden Gems

Published on September 19, 2025

This is my first blog post. In this blog, I want to share some online resources I’ve found valuable. They’re not always mainstream, but they’ve been useful in their own way for me, from learning resources to CTF and security platforms.

Books

Let's say you want a certain book but you don't have the money to purchase one. You can visit Libgen, paste the book name, and download it. It's an incredible resource for knowledge seekers.

Games

Tuttop is a well-known source for PC games. It's a great place to find a vast library of titles, especially if you're looking to try before you buy.

Affordable VPS Providers

Finding a good, cheap VPS can be tough. Here is a list of affordable providers I've used: PTYS, Northhost, and OVCloud. Each offers different pricing, with some plans starting as low as $2.50/month for 4GB RAM and 2 CPU cores.

CTF and Security Platforms

Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges and security labs are some of the best ways to build practical skills. OverTheWire was my first real entry point into CTFs, and it taught me how to think like an attacker. WebSec Dojo focuses on web security, which is crucial for modern applications. Pwn.college takes it further, offering structured lessons in exploitation and advanced security topics.
